Environmental Policy

Last Updated: September 15, 2025

At The Crowne, we are dedicated to minimizing our environmental impact and promoting sustainable practices. This Environmental Policy outlines our commitment to environmental responsibility in all operations, ensuring a greener future for our guests, staff, and the community in Accra.

1. Our Commitment

We strive to reduce our carbon footprint, conserve resources, and support local environmental initiatives. Our goal is to operate in an eco-friendly manner while providing exceptional hospitality.

2. Energy Conservation

We implement energy-efficient measures including:

  • LED lighting throughout the property.
  • Smart thermostats to optimize heating and cooling.
  • Encouraging guests to reuse linens and towels to reduce laundry energy use.

3. Water Management

Water conservation is a priority:

  • Low-flow fixtures in bathrooms and kitchens.
  • Regular maintenance to prevent leaks.
  • Awareness programs for guests on water-saving practices.

4. Waste Reduction and Recycling

We aim to minimize waste:

  • Comprehensive recycling programs for paper, plastics, and organics.
  • Reducing single-use plastics by providing reusable alternatives.
  • Composting food waste where possible.

5. Sustainable Sourcing

We support local and sustainable suppliers:

  • Sourcing locally grown produce to reduce transportation emissions.
  • Partnering with eco-certified vendors for amenities and supplies.

6. Community and Biodiversity

Our rooftop terrace and green spaces promote biodiversity. We participate in local clean-up events and educate guests on environmental awareness.

7. Monitoring and Improvement

We regularly audit our practices and set targets for improvement. Guest feedback is valued to enhance our sustainability efforts.
